About me

I graduated Computer Science and Biomedical Engineering. I like programming in all its various applications. I enjoy basic electronics, like to implement projects on Aruino or Teensy. Small robots and vehicles are one of my biggest hobbies. Thankfully usually vision processing is a key to many projects, as that is my another passion. I often use processing of 2D and 3D images requiring simple algorithms (based on openCV) or convolutional neural networks.

Currently I am involved in teachning programming which surprisingly for me is what I enjoy in work and gives a lot of profound insight and satisfaction. Teaching through both profound automated verification of students' answers and individual guiding through course allows to expect high quality programmers.
